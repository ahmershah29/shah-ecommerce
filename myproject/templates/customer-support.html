Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

You can track your order by going to your account and clicking on ‘Track My Order.’ Enter your order ID to view its status.

What are your shipping options?

We offer standard and express shipping options. Delivery times vary based on your location.

How can I return a product?

You can return a product within 30 days of purchase. Please visit our returns page for more details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.

How do I reset my password?

If you have forgotten your password, click on the Forgot Password link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we will send you a link to reset your password.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team via phone at +92 331-4413 874 or email at ahmarshah170@gmail.com.

How do I update my account information?

To update your account information, log into your account and navigate to the Profile section. Here, you can edit your details and save the changes.

What is your privacy policy?

Our privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. You can read the full policy on our Privacy Policy page.

How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription, log into your account and navigate to the Subscriptions section. Here, you can manage your subscriptions and cancel them if needed.

What is your exchange policy?

Our exchange policy allows you to exchange products within 30 days of purchase. The items must be in their original condition and packaging. Contact our support team to initiate an exchange.
